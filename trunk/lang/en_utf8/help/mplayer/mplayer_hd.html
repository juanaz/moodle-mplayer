JW FLV Player HD Video URL

HD Video URL: The URL path to an alternative high definition video file or stream.

Please note: When a URL link is present in this field, the HD plugin from LongtailVideo.com is automatically loaded and an "HD is on/off" dock will appear at the top right of the JW FLV Player window, allowing users to switch between HD and normal definition video streams.
